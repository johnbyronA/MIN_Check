Objective

To make use of the AI image recognition tools of the Chat GPT 4+ model to achieve integration of its services with our company, thereby creating a model that develops and coexists with the business rules of the company. This will generate a new productive process for evaluating MIN times in the recognition of images sent by users, with the goal of improving customer service and enhancing the productivity of our team members.

FAQs

The classification system evaluates several key factors including background uniformity, facial positioning, expression, and the presence of face accessories like earrings and glasses. A photo will be classified as MIN if it fails to meet the specified standards in these areas.

The GPT-4 Vision API analyzes images based on Moroccan visa criteria, such as background uniformity, facial visibility, and accessories, to classify them as MIN or NON MIN.

Customers benefit from the API's precise feedback, which guides them in submitting compliant photos, increasing their chances of successful Moroccan visa applications without delays.

The GPT-4 Vision API streamlines the visa image evaluation process, enabling employees to quickly identify photos that meet or fail Moroccan visa requirements, thus increasing workflow efficiency.

The training will primarily focus on familiarizing staff with the API's interface and functionality, including how to submit images for analysis and interpret the classification results. Additionally, staff will be educated on understanding the criteria used by the API to classify images as MIN or NON MIN, enabling them to effectively communicate these standards to customers.
